Travel By Plane

The Hartsfield-Jackson International Airport is a short 10-mile drive from downtown Atlanta and all the wedding action.

The wedding hotels are both accessible from the airport via MARTA, Atlanta's train system. You can take MARTA from the airport to the PeachTree Center Station, just steps aways from the hotels.
